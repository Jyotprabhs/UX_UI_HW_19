About Me



I am an Indian UX designer currently residing in Ohio, USA.


When I started cooking at the age of 14, my design career officially began. When I made my first dish with an Indo-Chinese fusion, magic happened. Cooking helped me become more agile in my learning, and it also helped me enhance my analytical and quantitative thinking. For me, innovation and creativity began in the kitchen. Over the course of seven years, I worked in the education department and taught more than 100 students while pursuing my bachelor's and master's degrees in computer science and mathematics. My intimate work with students aided in the development of my "Empathy." After my marriage, I relocated to Ohio, USA. I shifted into UX design with the desire to rediscover my creative side. I made the decision to pursue a career in ux design, and I'm now applying my skills to fix issues and improve user experiences.


I make baked goods for my family when I'm not at work. Fun fact about me: My kitchen is 90% plastic free.
